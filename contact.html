Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I care for my new plant?

Each plant comes with detailed care instructions specific to its species. Generally, ensure proper lighting, water when the soil feels dry, and maintain appropriate humidity levels. Our care guides include specific watering schedules, light requirements, and seasonal care tips.

What's your shipping policy?

We offer free shipping on orders over $50. Plants are carefully packaged and shipped within 1-2 business days. Delivery typically takes 2-5 business days depending on your location. We use specialized plant shipping materials to ensure your plants arrive healthy and happy.

Can I return a plant if it's not thriving?

Yes! We offer a 30-day plant guarantee. If your plant isn't thriving despite following our care instructions, we'll provide a replacement or full refund. Contact our support team with photos and we'll help troubleshoot or arrange a return.

Do you offer plant consultation services?

Absolutely! Our plant experts are available for virtual consultations to help you choose the right plants for your space and lifestyle. We also offer on-site consultations for bulk orders and large installations.

How do I send a plant as a gift?

Simply select any plant and choose the "Send as Gift" option. You can include a personalized message, choose the delivery date, and we'll include beautiful gift packaging. The recipient will receive care instructions and a note about the gift.
