About PlantGift

Connecting people with nature through the joy of plant gifting

Our Mission

At PlantGift, we believe that plants have the power to transform spaces, improve wellbeing, and strengthen connections between people.

Founded in 2020, we started with a simple idea: make it easy for anyone to share the gift of nature with their loved ones. What began as a small local initiative has grown into a passionate community of plant lovers dedicated to spreading green joy across communities.

Our Story

PlantGift was born from a simple moment of joy â€“ watching a friend's face light up when receiving their first houseplant. That small act of giving sparked an idea that would grow into something much larger.

Our founders, Sarah and Marcus, were urban gardening enthusiasts who noticed how plants transformed not just spaces, but people's moods and wellbeing. They started sharing plants with friends and neighbors, and soon realized the powerful impact of plant gifting on communities.

What started in a small apartment has now grown into a thriving business that has delivered over 50,000 plants to happy recipients across the country. But we've never lost sight of our original mission: spreading joy and connection through the simple, beautiful act of giving plants.
